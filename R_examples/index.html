Chapter 1 Introduction

This book contains a series of R examples that are often required to meet real life use cases. The general format is as follows:

  • Introduce the capability.
  • Briefly describe the key packages available.
  • Introduce a toy practice dataset that can easily be replicated on any machine.
  • Run one or two key functions that demonstrate the capability.
  • Show the results.

The examples will generally follow a tidyverse based approach: https://www.tidyverse.org/.
